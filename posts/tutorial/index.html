Secure accounts

Keeping your privacy by having secure accounts
and doing minimal less is more on the Internet.

Follow these steps:

  1. Use a password manager. (eg.: KeyPass)
  2. Create unique and long passwords. (change your passwords now)
  3. Delete unneeded accounts - stay minimal.
  4. Consider using secure browsers aswell! (eg.: Firefox, Brave, Tor)
